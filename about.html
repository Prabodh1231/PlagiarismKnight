How It Works

PlagCheck operates entirely on your device - no internet connection required. The entire process happens locally, ensuring your work remains completely private.

Upload Your Document

Add Reference Materials

Upload reference materials that your work might be compared against. These could be course materials, research papers, or other sources.

Run The Check

Our algorithm analyzes your document against the reference materials to identify potential similarities.

Receive a detailed report highlighting potential matches and similarity percentages to help you ensure originality.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is PlagCheck compared to university tools? +

While PlagCheck provides a solid foundation for plagiarism detection, it's important to note that it doesn't have access to the extensive proprietary databases that university-provided tools often use. PlagCheck works by comparing your document against the reference materials you provide, making it extremely effective for checking against course materials but less comprehensive for detecting similarities with published works not included in your references.

Is my data really safe and private even with advertisements? +

Absolutely. PlagCheck operates entirely within your browser. There is no backend server processing your documents, no data storage, and no transmission of your content over the internet. Your documents never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security. The advertisements are loaded separately and have no access to your uploaded files or their content.

What file formats does PlagCheck support? +

PlagCheck supports most common document formats including .docx, .pdf, .txt, .rtf, .odt, and more. The application extracts text content from these files for analysis.

Is there an ad-free version available? +

Currently, we only offer the ad-supported version of PlagCheck. We've made this decision to keep the service completely free for all users while maintaining its privacy-first approach. We carefully select our ad partners and ensure the advertisements are non-intrusive and clearly separated from the plagiarism checking functionality.

Can I use PlagCheck for my professional work? +

While primarily designed for students, PlagCheck can be used by anyone looking to verify the originality of their written content. However, for professional publications or critical documents, we recommend using it as a preliminary check before employing more comprehensive commercial solutions.
